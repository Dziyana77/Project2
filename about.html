Useless Books

What kind of a silly early web developers website did I get my self into you ask? Well, this e-commerce site has a simple idea to have an easy way of selling your expensive textbooks from college. Yea those huge books that take up all of the space in your house. You bought them during your college days hoping that they will help. Well, maybe they actually did not in the case of a Computer Science major, where a professor asks you to buy a huge expensive book about JAVA and all you did that semester, was open at once. Something happens to most of my college textbooks. Sound familiar well this site will help you to get rid of this problem, it will allow you to post your used books here and get them into the right hands that will actually use them. And if you're looking for used textbook you can find them right here.
